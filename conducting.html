Conducting style

When I work towards a performance, I prefer to do it with the orchestra's own people (the permanent core). As little as possible with substitutes, because: after the concert / competition you have to rely on the regular group again and in my view it is disappointing if you have had a 'nice' concert and then you can no longer perform the repertoire properly. carry out, because you miss a lot of people.


Availability

To check the availability of Bert to support your orchestra/project it is best to reach out through the contact form here. Schedules will need to be checked and ideally plans are made in advance. Regarding rehearsals or sick leave cover availability can be "last-minute".


For the type of projects, I have a wide availability. My working area would mostly be in the province of North-Holland. But I could be available for concert orchestra's, brass bands, youth ensembles and wind ensembles. I have conducted all ages so I adapt my conducting style accordingly.
